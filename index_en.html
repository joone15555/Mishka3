    About Us

    Mishkat was designed to be your simple map of Mecca, so you can enjoy a quick and clear experience in Mecca.



    Features

    Interactive Maps

    Provides an interactive map service that visitors to the Holy House of God can interact with. These maps show them the following: restrooms, a restaurant, a hospital, a pharmacy, an ATM, currency exchange and barber shops, a supermarket, a shopping mall, etc.

    Supports multiple languages and people with special needs

    Provides a service for non-Arabic users around the world to facilitate the program's use, as well as for people with special needs.

    Doesn't require internet access

    We offer offline access to the program if the visitor doesn't have internet access.
